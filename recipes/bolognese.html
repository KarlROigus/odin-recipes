Bolognese

Description

This dish can be made using flour and minced meat + tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Minced meat
  • Flour
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Make spaghetti from the flour
  2. Fry the minced meat with onion and garlic
  3. Put tomato sauce and bring it to boil
  4. Mix everything up
